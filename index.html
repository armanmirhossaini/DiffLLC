Meet The Founder

I have always had a keen interest in entrepreneurialism, which led to starting Diff LLC in hope to create something useful for society. I felt academia has failed students in the sense that there are heavy communication barriers for Peer-to-Peer collaboration, compromising student's academic sucess. My intent is to erode at these barriers and create a Decentralized Virtual Collaboration Platform exclusively for students that is preplatformed and preorganized per their university and class enrollment status.
